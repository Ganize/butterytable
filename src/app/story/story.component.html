Our Story

At Butterytable bakery, we aim to serve customised cakes and desserts that are lower in sugar. Our cakes are hand-baked to perfection and meticulously coated with buttercream and fondant alike, using only the finest ingredients which are free from any preservatives.

We are in the business of happiness and it is our goal to bring joy to you and your loved ones with our creations, constantly innovating new designs and flavours.

We bake for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, dessert tables and more.

You can contact us at our quotation/inquiry form for any orders/inquiries.
